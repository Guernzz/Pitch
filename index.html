The Umbrella Corporation is a multinational conglomerate with subsidiaries active in a variety of industries from the 1980s to current day. Umbrella has influence in the production and sale of cosmetics, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, industrial machine production, consumer products, health foods, the transportation industry and tourism.

OUR MISSION:

To ensure everyone possible a reliable, effective and affordable vaccine for Covid-19.

NUMBER OF SHOTS:

2 shots, 21 days apart Moderately to severely immunocompromised people ages 12 years and older should get an additional primary shot at least 28 days after their second shot.

BOOSTER SHOT:

Everyone ages 18 years and older is eligible to get a booster shot at least 6 months after completing their Pfizer primary series. You can get any of the COVID-19 vaccines authorized in the United States for your booster shot.
